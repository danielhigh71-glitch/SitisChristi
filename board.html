Board of Directors

Focused on practical intervention, clinical care, and effective humanitarian service.

The Board of Directors of Sitis Christi will be composed of individuals selected for their relevant expertise, judgment, and commitment to the organization’s work. Each member will bring a practical perspective from medicine, faith leadership, or international humanitarian service. The board seats listed below will represent roles being filled as Sitis Christi prepares to expand its reach.

Current Seats

Three seats. Three areas of expertise.

The board seats listed below represent roles currently being filled as Sitis Christi prepares to expand its reach.

Board Seat No. 1
🩺

Medical Professional

Physician Surgeon Prosthetist Allied Medical Expert

Sitis Christi seeks a physician, surgeon, prosthetist, or allied medical professional who can help guide its prosthetics and medical intervention programs. This board seat is intended to provide clinical oversight, practical input, and informed review of program decisions. A medical voice at the board level helps ensure that services are realistic, responsible, and aligned with appropriate standards of care.

Board Seat No. 2
✝️

Clergy / Pastoral Leadership

Pastor Priest Ordained Leader Chaplain

Sitis Christi seeks a pastor, priest, chaplain, or ordained leader who can help keep the organization connected to its faith-based purpose while supporting practical service in varied communities. This board member is provide perspective on mission, ethics, community trust, and pastoral care. The role is intended to help the organization remain clear in its identity and useful in its work.

Board Seat No. 3
🌍

International Humanitarian

Field Practitioner Aid Worker Global Development Specialist

Sitis Christi seeks an individual with direct experience in international humanitarian work, including service in refugee settings, post-conflict areas, underserved communities, or related field environments. This board member is help guide program strategy, partnerships, logistics, and long-term impact. Their field experience is help ensure that plans are practical and responsive to the conditions in which the work takes place.
