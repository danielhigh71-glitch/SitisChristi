About Us

What We Believe

"Rooted in Christ’s words"

‘I thirsted and you gave me water.’

Four ideas behind everything we do.

✦

We respond to needs

We believe some people have no help out there at all. No safety net, no backup plan, and no one coming. Our work begins with them.

◆

We are that somebody

People often see a problem and say, "Somebody should do something." We believe that if we can help, we should. We are that somebody.

○

You don't have to wait for permission to show up

You do not need a title, platform, or official invitation to notice a need and respond. Sometimes the next right step is simply to go, listen, and take action where help is actually useful.

⚯

Lives turn on small hinges

We aim to use our resources where they can make the largest difference with the smallest necessary amount. Small sums carry very different weight in different parts of the world.

✦Sitis ChristiLives turn on small hinges

A faith-based humanitarian effort funding small, timely interventions for people at the edge of survival — in India, Sierra Leone, and Mexico.
